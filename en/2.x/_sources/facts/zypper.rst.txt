Zypper Facts
------------

:code:`zypper.ZypperRepositories`
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

.. code:: python

    host.get_fact(ZypperRepositories)


Returns a list of installed zypper repositories:

    [
